Bio

I recently received my Master of Public Health and Master of Social Work degree and work as a data analyst/scientist in Washington DC. My skills lie at the cross-section of data analysis, visualization,, and reporting, and I am dedicated to telling stories with data that appeal to audiences regardless of social educational background. I also hold skills in creating interactive dashboards for audiences to see their data come alive! My research areas of interest are in social determinants of health, health disparities, chronic illness, and disabilities. I have experience working with a diverse range of behavioral and biomedical research teams, and I embrace a collaborative and engaging style of data management. I am currently working to build my knowledge in neural network topology in Python and R for data analysis, image process, and image/audio creation. My current data projects involve exploring neural networks’ use in image and sound processing.
